Available Discounts

We offer several discounts to make travel more affordable for various groups. See below for more details:

  • University Student Discount: 20% off

    This discount is available for full-time university students. To avail of this discount, students must present a valid student ID at the time of booking or boarding.

  • Child Discount: 50% off

    This discount is available for children between the ages of 5 and 12. Proof of age may be required.

  • Employee Discount: 25% off

    Exclusive to IreBus employees. A valid employee ID must be presented when purchasing tickets or boarding.

  • Elderly Discount: 30% off

    Available for senior citizens aged 65 and over. Proof of age (such as a government-issued ID) must be presented at the time of travel.

  • Resident Discount: 15% off

    Residents of Sligo County are eligible for this discount. Proof of residence (e.g., a utility bill or official correspondence) is required.
